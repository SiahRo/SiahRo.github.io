BLT Grilled Cheese Recipe

Preparation time: 5 Minutes

Ingredients

  • Sliced Cheese
  • Sliced Bread
  • Sliced Tomato
  • BLT
  • Butter

Preparation

  1. Fry your bacon.
  2. Butter your toast.
  3. Place cheese in bread.
  4. Place tomatoes in bread.
  5. Place the bacon in bread.
  6. Fry for 5 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  7. Serve warm!
